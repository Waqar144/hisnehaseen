ar: اَمْسَيْنَا وَ اَمْسيَ الْمُلْكُ لِلّٰهِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ اَعُوْذُ بِاللّٰهِ الَّذِيْ يُمْسِكُ السَّمَائَ اَنْ تَقَعَ عَلَي الْارْضِ اِلاَّ بِِاِذْنِهٖ مِنْ شَرِّ مَاخَلَقَ وَذَرَأَ وَبَرَأَ

"We enter upon the evening and so does the dominion for Allah's sake, and praise belongs to Allah. I seek refuge in Allah, who withholds the heaven lest it should fall upon the earth except by His leave. From the mischief of that which he has created, spread out and fashioned into existence."
ref:(Tabarani in al-Kabeer, as narrated by Ibn Umar)

1: Let him say
ar: بِسْمِ اللّه
ref: (Tirmidhi 25, Ibn Majah 398, Abu Dawood 101, as narrated by Abu Hurayrah R.A & Saeed Ibn Zayd R.A)
---
2: And say:

ar: اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي ذَنْبِي، وَوَسِّعْ لِي فِي دَارِي، وَبَارِكْ لِي فِيمَا رَزَقْتَنِي

"O Allah, forgive me my sins, and make my home spacious for me, and grant me blessings in my provision"

ref: (Tirmidhi 3500, Tabarani Awsat 974 narrated by Abu Hurayrah R.A)

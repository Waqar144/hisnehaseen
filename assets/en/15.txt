1:
ar: الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَقَالَنَا يَوْمَنَا هَذَا وَلَمْ يُهْلِكْنَا بِذُنُوبِنَا
All praise belongs to Allah who let us see this day as forgiven ones and did not destroy us because of our sins.
(Muslim 822/1911 narrated by Ibn Mas'ud)
---
2:
ar: اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّهِ الَّذِيْ وَهَبَنا هٰذَا الْيَوْمَ ، وَأَقَالَنَا فِيْهِ عَثراتِنَا، وَلَمْ يُعَذِّبْنَا بِالنَّارِ
"All praise belongs to Allah who has let us have this day and forgave us our mistakes and protected us from the punishment of the Fire." (Tabarani in Kabeer, Ibn Sunni, narrated by Ibn Ma'ood)
---
3: Then offer two raka'ah Salah
(Tirmidhi 586 narrated: Anas. Tabarani al-Kabeer, narrated by Abu Umamah.)
---
4: According to a Hadith Qudsi, Allah said, "O son of Adam, offer four raka'ah” in the first portion of the day. That would be enough for you till the end of the day."
(Tirmidhi 475)

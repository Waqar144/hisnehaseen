ar: اللَّهُمَّ هَذَا إِقْبَالُ لَيْلِكَ وَإِدْبَارُ نَهَارِكَ وَأَصْوَاتُ دُعَاتِكَ فَاغْفِرْ لِي

O Allah, this is the hour of the advent of your night and the retreat of your day and of the cry of your petitioners. So, forgive me!
ref:(Abu Dawood 530, Tirmidhi 3589, Haakim 714 as narrated by Umm Salamah)

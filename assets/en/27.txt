1: He must say  بِسْمِ الله (In the Name of Allah) (while he is still outside the latrine)

ref: (Ibn Abu Shaybah, Ibn Sunni, narrated by Ali R.A).
---
2: And if he goes to the privy (toilet), say:

ar:  اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْخُبُثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ

O Allah, I seek your protection against the foul male and female Jinns.

ref: (Bukhari 142, Muslim 375, Tirmidhi 5, Abu Dawood 6, narrated by Anas R.A)
---
3: When he comes out of toilet, say:

ar: غُفْرَانَكَ

(Oh Allah I need) "Your forgiveness!"
ref: (Tirmidhi 7, Abu Dawood 30, Ibn Majah 300)
---
4: And make this supplication:

ar: الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَذْهَبَ عَنِّي الْأَذٰى وَعَافَانِي

"Praise be to Allah who relieved me from the suffering and gave me health."
ref: (Nasa'i, Ibn Sunni, narrated by Abu Zarr R.A)
